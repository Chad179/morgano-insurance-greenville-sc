Personal and business insurance questions often overlap, but they should not be lumped together. This guide helps readers separate household coverage, business coverage, and proof-of-insurance needs.

Which questions belong under personal insurance?

Personal insurance questions usually involve drivers, homes, renters, life insurance, motorcycles, flood exposure, and umbrella liability.

Which questions belong under business insurance?

Business questions usually involve contracts, certificates, employees, payroll, vehicles used for work, professional mistakes, property, and cyber risk.

Which questions belong under proof of insurance?

Certificate requests, SR-22 filings, lease requirements, and lender requirements are proof-of-coverage questions. The details must match the policy.
